SVCC 2023 T13 Demo page
=======================

Demo page of the T13 systems for `singing voice conversion challenge (SVCC) 2023 <https://arxiv.org/abs/2306.14422>`_. 

.. note:: 

   If you are using dark mode with your OS, please switch to the light theme using the button on the top right for better visibility. Headphones are recommended for listening.

Paper
-----

Submitted to `ASRU 2023 <http://www.asru2023.org/>`_

A Comparative Study of Voice Conversion Models with Large-Scale Speech and Singing Data: The T13 Systems for the Singing Voice Conversion Challenge 2023
